Writing & Rhetoric

Documents focused on an understanding of primary resources, theories, and research in the fields of arts and philosophy -- mainly writing and rhetoric.

On Athenian Law

This document is an analysis of ancient Athenian political culture via translated primary sources, secondary sources, and other peer-reviewed materials.

Permaculture: Approaches To A Sustainable Future

This work explored permaculture and some of its proposed solitions to environmental sustainability while diving into issues with current agricultural practices. All evidence came from peer-reviewed sources.

Love & Authenticity

This document analyzed the phenomenological theories of authenticity as developed by historical figures Sartre and De Beauvoir and how it applies to love, concluding with a critique and a way of moving forward.

A Visual Model Of Rhetorical Ecology

This work is intended for an audience with an education level anywhere from middle school to high school. It's intended to describe complex ideas of rhetoric to the youth, and focuses on scaffolding difficult ideas while presenting them in an eaisly digestable fashion.

Heracles & Greatness

This piece analyzed the story of Heracles as presented in two different time periods and the cultural differences of those time periods, resuting in different values and ideas being put forward to the viewers.
