Writing and Mathematics

is where Elijah locates himself. Driven by technical work, he has a B.A. of Writing & Rhetoric and a minor in Mathematics at the University of Central Florida where his expected graduation date is December 2024.

During his time in Writing & Rhetoric he enjoyed aspects of project management, holding roles such as the Art Director of a student magazine, and the General Editor of a writing manual. Elijah also grew fond of technical communication when he worked as a Mentor Writing Consultant.

Within mathematics he has studied computer logic and algorithms, having now taken courses in Graph Theory, Machine Learning, and Statistical Learning. As such, Eli became fond of computer languages and has an understanding of Python, C, CSS, and HTML. Nevertheless, he remains curious about math, and has even explored a masters-level course in Mathematics for Engineers.

Featured Works

Cypress Dome Magazine

Elijah worked as the Art Director alongside other leadership to create the Spring 2022 publication of the Cypress Dome Magazine.

In particular, he facilitated the curation of art for the magazine with his team of Art Screeners, helped make decisions about the magazine's artistic design, and worked closely to promote and manage events.

The Cypress Dome Magazine is a student operated literary magazine that showcases students' creative work including Poetry, fiction, creative nonfiction, flash fiction/nonfiction, scripts, graphic narratives, and visual art.

UCF Marvel Writing Manual

Elijah worked as one of the General Editors of the UCF Marvel Manual, polishing the section Research & Documentation. He also worked as a production member of the Research & Documentation chapter.

The UCF Marvel Manual is an over 200 page work from a 26-person student team intended to be a writing manual for UCF students and is the final product of a Project Management course. The proect was completed over the Fall 2022 semester through asynchronous communication, and meetings regarding important decisions.
